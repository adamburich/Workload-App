Help and About

Workload is an app developed to help you stay organized and on top of upcoming assignments.

  • To add a new assignment, go to dashboard and click add assignment then submit the following form.
  • To mark an assignment as completed, go to dashboard and click complete assignment.
  • To delete an assignment, go dashboard and click delete assignment.
  • To delete your account, go to settings and click delete account.
  • To view your completed assignments, go to settings and click view completed assignments.

If something isn't showing up refreshing will likely solve the problem.
